# 🇵🇰 GridX Pakistan – AI Energy Market Intelligence

An **AI-powered energy market simulation platform** built with **Streamlit** to analyze electricity demand, renewable generation, and energy prices.
The platform demonstrates **AI forecasting, battery arbitrage strategies, and real-time market condition detection** for modern power systems.

---

## ⚡ Key Features

📊 **Energy Market Dashboard**

* Visualize **Load, Solar, Wind, and Net Load trends**

🤖 **AI Price Forecasting**

* Predict **electricity prices for the next 24 hours** using a trained ML model

🔋 **Battery Arbitrage Simulator**

* Simulates **charge/discharge strategy** based on market prices
* Calculates **profit and state-of-charge (SOC)**

📈 **Energy Market Analytics**

* Average system load
* Renewable energy share
* Electricity price insights

🚦 **Market Condition Detection**
Automatically identifies market state:

* 🔴 **Scarcity Market** – High demand
* 🟢 **Surplus Market** – Excess supply
* 🔵 **Balanced Market**
